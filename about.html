Welcome

A BROOKLYN Tradition Built on Taste & Love

Slices by Smiles Douglas is a renowned custom cake bakery rooted in the BROOKLYN, NY. Founded by Douglas in 2000, we began in Queens where we saw a need and answered it — baking cakes that were so well-received our reputation spread across all five boroughs and beyond.

We are committed to delivering a distinct experience of freshness, quality, and supreme taste in every cake, while promoting healthy eating for a sustainable future. We strive to bring smiles to everyone's faces — and dedicate our time to mentoring youth and building a legacy for our community.

  • ✓ Custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, traditional holidays & special occasions
  • ✓ Only natural, high-quality ingredients — no shortcuts
  • ✓ Customer favorites: Blue Velvet Birthday Cake & Chocolate Ganache Cheesecake
  • ✓ Serving New York and the entire Tri-State area since 2000
  • ✓ Stress-free ordering with personal attention to every detail
